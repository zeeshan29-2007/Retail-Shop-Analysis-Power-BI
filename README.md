### üìà Analytical Components

#### **1. Sales by Region**

A pie chart visualizing revenue distribution across the West, Northeast, Southeast, Midwest, and South regions, enabling comparison of regional market performance.

#### **2. Top 3 Products**

A bar chart highlighting the top-performing product categories based on revenue. This helps identify strong product lines and prioritize inventory or marketing decisions.

#### **3. Sales by Retailers**

A donut chart illustrating revenue contribution from key retail partners such as One Sports, West Gear, Sport Locker, and others, supporting retailer performance evaluations.

#### **4. Top 5 Customers**

A horizontal bar chart ranking customers based on purchase volume, helping identify high-value clients for targeted retention strategies.

#### **5. Sales by State**

An interactive geospatial map (powered by Bing Maps) showing sales concentration across U.S. states, useful for location-based analysis and regional targeting.

#### **6. Monthly Sales Trend**

A line chart visualizing month-over-month revenue performance, enabling the detection of seasonal patterns, peak periods, and trends.
